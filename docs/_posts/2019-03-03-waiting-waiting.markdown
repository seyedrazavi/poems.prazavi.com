![image](/assets/images/waiting.jpeg)

Satisfying click
the belt straps in
going for a ride
mittens on tight
waiting for mamma
throwing strange faces
wrinkles in time
chewing our tongues
turned from the sun
glaring, 
crying out
nana nana
waiting for the boy
fetching a toy
telling stories
mama in tow
here they come!
here they come!
lets go lets go!
